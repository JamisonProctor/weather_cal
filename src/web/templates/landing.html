Questions?

What is WeatherCal?
WeatherCal delivers a 14-day weather forecast directly into your calendar app as events. Each day shows an emoji summary of morning and afternoon conditions — no app to open, no website to check.
How do I add it to my calendar?
After signing up and choosing your city, you'll get a personal feed URL. Tap it on your phone or paste it into your calendar app's "subscribe" option. Your calendar will automatically pull in new forecasts.
Which calendar apps are supported?
WeatherCal works with any app that supports ICS calendar subscriptions — including Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, and most others. For Google Calendar on Android, we also offer a direct Google sync option.
How often does the forecast update?
Forecasts are refreshed every morning. Your calendar app will pick up the latest data automatically — most apps check every few hours.
Can I change my location?
Yes — go to Settings and update your city at any time. Your forecast feed will switch to the new location on the next refresh.
How do I change temperature units?
In Settings, you can switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit. The change applies to all your forecast events.
Is my data private?
Yes. We store only what's needed to generate your forecast: your email, location, and preferences. We don't sell data or show ads. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Is it free?
Yes, WeatherCal is completely free.
