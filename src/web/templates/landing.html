WeatherCal ☀️

Free Saturday — but is the weather? WeatherCal puts 14-day forecasts directly in your calendar, so you never have to switch apps to make a plan.

  • ☀️Emoji-first forecasts — morning and afternoon conditions as all-day calendar events, readable at a glance
  • ⚠️Warnings where you'll actually see them — rain ☂️, wind 🌬️, cold 🥶, and snow ☃️ flag right in the event title
  • 🕐Timed warning events — when rain or wind is coming, a precise block event (e.g. "☂️ Rain Warning 10am–2pm") lands in your calendar so you know exactly when to pack an umbrella
  • 📋Hourly detail on demand — tap any event to see a full hour-by-hour breakdown with per-hour warnings
  • 📅Works everywhere — Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook — subscribe once, updated every morning
