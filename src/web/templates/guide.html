Think of the title as the quick glance and this as the full picture — everything you need to plan your day.

We'll also let you know when the weather gets rough.

When rain, wind, snow, or extreme temperatures are in the forecast, the emoji in your daily summary changes to give you a heads-up. Try toggling these to see how it works:

☂️ Rain ❄️ Snow 🌬️ Wind 🥶 Cold 🥵 Heat
☀️12° → ☀️18°C

The warning emoji replaces the normal weather emoji for that half of the day. So if it's going to rain in the morning, you'll see ☂️ instead of ☀️ — at a glance, before you even open the event.

For cold and heat warnings, you decide what counts as "cold" or "hot":

Want to know exactly when the bad weather hits?
