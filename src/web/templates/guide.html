Your weather, in your calendar.

WeatherCal puts a daily forecast directly in your calendar. No app to open — just glance at your day and you'll know what to expect.

Let's set yours up. About 2 minutes.

Your daily forecast

Each day, a forecast event appears at the top of your calendar. Here's what it looks like:

Tuesday, March 24
☁️8° → ☀️14°C
9 AM
Team standup
12 PM
Lunch with Sarah

The two temperatures are morning and afternoon, not high and low. The emoji shows the weather for each half of the day.

Open it for the full picture

When you click on the forecast event in your calendar, you'll see the weather broken down across five parts of the day:

☁️8° → ☀️14°C
Morning ☁️ 6°C
Midday ⛅ 10°C
Afternoon ☀️ 14°C
Evening ☁️ 11°C
Night ☁️ 7°C
⚙️ weathercal.app/settings

The title is your quick glance. This is the full detail — everything you need to plan your day.

Weather warnings

When rain, snow, wind, or extreme temperatures are expected, the emoji in your forecast changes to warn you. Toggle these to see:

☂️ Rain ❄️ Snow 🌬️ Wind 🥶 Cold 🥵 Heat
☀️12° → ☀️18°C

You control when cold and heat warnings trigger:

Changing your settings later

Every forecast event has a settings link at the bottom. Open any forecast in your calendar and look for this:

☁️8° → ☀️14°C
Morning ☁️ 6°C
Afternoon ☀️ 14°C
⚙️ weathercal.app/settings

You can also redo this guide anytime from your settings page.
